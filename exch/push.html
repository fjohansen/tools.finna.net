How to setup Push notifications

Follow these steps to get notifications

If you already have Pushover installed and configured, just press the Subscribe button below.

  • Download and install Pushover from App Store.
  • Open the app and press Create Account.
  • Enter your email and create a password. Then press Create Account.
  • Give your device a name (ex: 'Johns iPhone') and press Register Device.
  • Press the Subscribe button below to subscribe to push notifications from Finns Euro.
